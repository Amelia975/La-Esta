DINING


Dine-in & Chase the Flavours




La-Esta has three selection of restaurants and bars sparkle with the unmistakable vibrancy of Sri Lanka, Savour exceptional cuisine and rare wines in the most stylish spaces in town a thrilling taste of the city’s diverse culinary landscape.

ZHAO

INSPIRED CHINESE-PERUVIAN CUISINE Be captivated by the cosmopolitan buzz of Nobu, where you’ll delight in the world-famous fusion of Japanese and Peruvian cuisine invented by Michelin-starred master chef, Nobuyuki Matsuhisa. With stunning views of iconic Table Mountain, our Cape Town fine dining restaurant and chic bar comes alive with innovative dishes, intriguing cocktails, sparkling conversation and chilled-out tunes.

EXPLORE

99 BOTTELS BAR AND LOUNGE

The stylish heart of our resort, 99 BOTTELS Bar and Lounge’s eclectic drinks, live entertainment and unfettered mountain views are renowned in SRI LANKA. Indulge in afternoon tea, and by night, let our expert mixologists personalise your cocktail. Table Mountain forms an irresistible backdrop to your cosmopolitan evening out, as 99 BOTTELS buzzes with a sophisticated crowd.

EXPLORE

LOUIS

Ultra-cool Louis takes chill-out sessions to the next level with its irresistible fusion of indigo infinity pool, relaxed vibes and delectable fresh summer menu. Stretch out in your sun-lounger or beneath private, poolside cabanas, order your favourite beachy cocktail and while away the day at your own pace.

PRIVATE DINING

Anything is possible when you co-create with One&Only Cape Town's private dining team. Under their expert eye, your dream dining experience will be seamlessly fulfilled to your exact vision. Whether you desire an unforgettable, intimate dinner for two framed by the majestic Table Mountain, a relaxed braai – the traditional South African Barbeque – with friends and family by the water, or an in-suite breakfast extravaganza, we’ll make it happen.
