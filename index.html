ABOUT




La-Esta is located near Ella, close proximity to popular tourist attractions, hiking trails and many more. In addition to that,
La-Esta claims to posses the best view of the " Ella Gap". Ella is a small village in the highlands of Sri Lanka which is filled with tea estates, mountains, waterfalls and of course with some good air to breath. Lots of people make Ella as one of their must visit destination in srilanka just to witness the breathtaking views it creates. The destination enjoys 23C -30C weather conditions all year round. is adorned by several stunning waterfalls amidst lush green mountains. Ella is home to the famous Ravana falls, Ravana caves, little Adams peak and the nine arches bridge.
